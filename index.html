1. Geetha Govindam - 113.2 million & BOX OFFICE COLLECTION : ₹ 130 crore 🎬

Geetha Govindam –Geetha Govindam 2018 is a Telugu romantic comedy starring Vijay Deverakonda and Rashmika Mandanna. The plot follows Vijay Govind, a responsible young lecturer, who falls for the level-headed Geetha. A misunderstanding during a bus journey leads Geetha to believe Vijay is a pervert, causing her to despise him. Chaos ensues when they realize they are connected through family—Geetha’s brother is engaged to Vijay’s sister. Despite her hatred, they are forced to interact during wedding preparations, where Vijay struggles to prove his innocence and win her heart. Through a series of comedic, heartfelt events and misunderstandings, Geetha realizes her mistake and falls for him. The film concludes with them overcoming obstacles and starting their journey together, highlighting themes of patience, respect, and love.

2. Salaar- 108.5 million & BOX OFFICE COLLECTION : ₹ 120 crore 🎬

Salaar – Ceasefire is a 2023 Indian Telugu-language epic action thriller film directed by Prashanth Neel and produced by Vijay Kiragandur under Hombale Films. The film stars Prabhas in the titular role, alongside Prithviraj Sukumaran, Shruti Haasan, Jagapathi Babu, Bobby Simha, Sriya Reddy, Ramachandra Raju, John Vijay, Easwari Rao, Tinnu Anand, Devaraj, Brahmaji and Mime Gopi. In the fictional dystopian city-state of Khansaar, where monarchy still exists, the film follows the friendship between Deva (Prabhas), the exiled prince of Khansaar, and Varadha (Prithviraj Sukumaran), the current prince of Khansaar. When a coup d'état is planned by his father's ministers and his relatives, Varadha enlists Deva's help to become Khansaar's undisputed ruler.The film's initial storyline was pitched from Neel's debut film Ugramm (2014) and is the maiden part of a two-part film.[4] It was officially announced in December 2020 under the title Salaar, however, in July 2023, its first installment was titled as Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire. Principal photography commenced in January 2021, and occurred sporadically in several legs over nearly three years, before wrapping in late 2023. Filming locations included Telangana, Italy and Budapest. Production difficulties, ranging from the pandemic, reshoots and VFX delays, postponed Salaar's release date several times. The music is composed by Ravi Basrur, cinematography handled by Bhuvan Gowda and editing by Ujwal Kulkarni.Part 1 – Ceasefire was theatrically released on 22 December 2023, coinciding with Christmas. The film received positive reviews from critics. It was a commercial success, earning ₹614 crore on a ₹270 crore budget making it the highest-grossing Telugu film of 2023, third highest-grossing Telugu film of all time, and the nineteenth highest-grossing Indian film of all time at the end of its theatrical run.[a] A sequel is in development.

3. They Call Him OG - 350 million & BOX OFFICE COLLECTION : ₹ 293 crore 🎬

OG -They Call Him OG" 2025 is a high-octane Telugu-language action-crime drama directed by Sujeeth, starring Pawan Kalyan as the titular character, Ojas Gambheera. After vanishing from the Mumbai underworld for a decade following a failed assassination attempt that killed his younger brother, Ojas resurfaces to seek vengeance against a new, corrupt coalition of crime lords. Returning from self-imposed exile in Southeast Asia, Ojas reunites with his loyal former enforcer, Parshuram, to dismantle the reign of rivals Vikram Shinde, tech-savvy drug trafficker Reza Khan, and political manipulator Malini Desai. The film focuses on the violent, stylish reclamation of his territory, featuring intense gun-and-sword combat sequences. The story is set largely in 1993 Bombay, focusing on themes of loyalty, revenge, and the return of a legendary gangster. The film is celebrated for its intense, fast-paced first half and powerful background score
